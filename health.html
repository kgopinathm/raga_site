Ayushman Bharath Free Health Insurance

Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana - PMJAY

  • Aadhar card must.(live photo to be taken)
  • All senior citizens aged 70 or above(as per Aadhar card)
  • Regardless of economic status.
  • Free Medical treatment up to Rs.5 lakhs per family/year.
  • Both Government and private hospitals empanelled under the Scheme.
  • Central and State Government cover annual premiums.
  • Find the network hospitals at hospitals.pmjay.gov.in
  • No seperate enrolment needed for each family member use add member feature in enrolment portal.
  • District Ayushman card will be issued.
  • Coverage starts immediately after registration.
  • Benefit only from one Insurance source cannot avail both simultaneously.
  • Once given option cannot be reversed.
