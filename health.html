New Health Insurance Scheme (NHIS)

  • 01.07.2022 - 30.03.2026 block of 4 years.
  • Coverage up to Rs.5.00 lakhs including spouse.
  • Special treatment upto Rs.10.00 lakhs.
  • Implemented through United India Insurance company.

Upper Limit of Medical Assistance

  • Cataract surgery up to Rs.30,000.
  • Uterus removal (Hysterectomy) Rs.50,000.
  • Reimbursement in non-network hospital: 75% of least package.
  • Claim within 1 month of discharge (grace period: 1 month).

Documents to be Enclosed – Non-Medical

  • Covering letter with address and contact number.
  • Common claim form.
  • PPO book or photo-affixed Insurance card.
  • Pensioner Aadhaar (both Aadhaar if claim for spouse).
  • Pension bank details.
  • PPO of family pensioner (if pensioner deceased).

Documents to be Enclosed – Medical

All originals required

  • Discharge summary.
  • Hospital final bills.
  • Detailed breakup of final bill.
  • Cash receipts.
  • Pharmacy bills with prescription.
  • Investigation reports (Lab/Scan).
  • Implant/stent invoices (if applicable).
  • Copy of FIR/MLC report in case of accident.

Ayushman Bharat Free Health Insurance

Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana – PMJAY

  • Aadhaar card must (live photo to be taken)
  • All senior citizens aged 70 or above (as per Aadhaar card)
  • Regardless of economic status
  • Free medical treatment up to Rs.5 lakhs per family/year
  • Both government and private hospitals empanelled under the scheme
  • Central and State Government cover annual premiums
  • Find hospitals at hospitals.pmjay.gov.in
  • No separate enrolment for each member; use add member feature
  • District Ayushman card will be issued
  • Coverage starts immediately after registration
  • Benefit from only one insurance source; cannot avail both simultaneously
  • Once given option cannot be reversed
